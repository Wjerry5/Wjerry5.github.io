About me

Welcome to my homepage! I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate from the Department of Computer Science at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC) . I obtained my Ph.D. from CS department at UIUC, advised by Prof. Tarek F. Abdelzaher. Prior to UIUC, I received my bachelor degree from Computer Science Department in Shanghai Jiao Tong University, under the supervision of Prof. Xinbing Wang and Prof. Weinan Zhang.

Research Interests

  • Graph Machine Learning: temporal graph learning, link prediction, temporal reasoning, few-shot learning

  • Efficient Graph Learning Platform: online graph learning, distributed training, real-time fine-tuning

  • Foundation Model for Multi-Modal Data: graph foudation model, multi-vantage signal pretraining, time-series analysis

  • Applications: recommendation, knowledge graph reasoning, social network analysis

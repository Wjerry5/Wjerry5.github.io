Bio

I am a Postdoctoral Research Associate from the Department of Computer Science at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC) . I obtained my Ph.D. from CS department at UIUC, advised by Prof. Tarek F. Abdelzaher. Prior to UIUC, I received my bachelor degree from Computer Science Department in Shanghai Jiao Tong University, under the supervision of Prof. Xinbing Wang and Prof. Weinan Zhang.

Research Interest

  • Graph Machine Learning: temporal graph learning, link prediction, temporal reasoning, few-shot learning
  • Efficient Graph Learning Platform : online graph learning, distributed training, real-time fine-tuning
  • Foundation Model for Multi-Modal Data: graph pretraining, multi-vantage pretraining , time-series analysis
  • Applications : recommendation, knowledge graph reasoning, social network analysis

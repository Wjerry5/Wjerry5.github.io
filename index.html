Bio

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, advised by Prof. Tarek F. Abdelzaher. Prior to UIUC, I received my bachelor degree from Computer Science Department in Shanghai Jiao Tong University, under the supervision of Prof. Xinbing Wang and Prof. Weinan Zhang.

Research Interest

My research interest includes machine learning and deep learning on graph, natural language, and time-series data, with applications on social network analysis, knowledge graph and dynamic systems.
